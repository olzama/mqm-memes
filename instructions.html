Evaluation Instructions

You are evaluating English subtitle translations of Russian film dialogue that has become culturally iconic ("memes"). For each item you will see the original Russian and one candidate translation.

Assess issues using two severity levels:

  • MAJOR — significantly harms meaning, humor, style, character voice, register, or other aspect of artistic effect. Example: a joke that is not funny at all in the translation. Note that this can be quite subtle, even punctuation can sometimes lead to loss of humorous or artistic effect. Generally, if the translation lacks character or is unclear to you, it means there is a major issue there.

Categories (for reference, not scored; do not worry if you are not sure which to choose): accuracy, fluency, style, terminology, other.

Notes:
